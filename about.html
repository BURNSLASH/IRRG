The IRRG is a fictional multinational paramilitary faction within Blackhawk Rescue Mission 5. Founded on 19 July 2024 by AzarothDiablo, the faction originated as a small, invitation-only collective of friends. Over time, its discipline, organization, and unified purpose led to its rise as one of the most active and respected forces within the Asia-Pacific sector of the PL5 community.
